Connect Your Domain

Point your domain to our global network by adding a single DNS record. This works for subdomains (e.g., pages.yourdomain.com) and root domains (e.g., yourdomain.com).

How to Add a DNS Record

  1. Log in to your DNS provider.
  2. Create a new record with the following values:
    Host/Name:

    Enter pages for a subdomain, or @ for the root domain.

    Type:

    Use CNAME for a subdomain. For a root domain, use ALIAS, ANAME, or CNAME Flattening.

    Target/Value:

    c.tracked.it

  3. Save the record. DNS changes usually take effect within a few minutes.

          FAQ
          
Frequently Asked Questions

        

        

          

            
              What kind of targeting can I use?
              
            
            
Our platform lets you target visitors based on location, device, browser, OS, traffic source parameters, referrer, and more. This allows you to show the right content to the right audience without managing multiple landing pages.

        

          

            
              Will this slow down my pages?
              
            
            
No, in fact, most users see faster load times. Your content is distributed globally and served from 300+ locations close to your visitors. All targeting decisions happen in milliseconds at the edge before the page is delivered.

        

          

            
              How does bot filtering work?
              
            
            
Our system automatically detects and filters suspicious traffic, ensuring your analytics remain clean and your pages are shown only to real users. This protects your ad spend and gives you more accurate performance data.
