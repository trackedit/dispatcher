Frequently Asked Questions

Is the free tier really free?
Yes! Our Starter plan allows up to 10,000 tracked events per month completely free of charge. No credit card required.
How does cookieless tracking work?
We use advanced digital fingerprinting and server-side signal correlation to attribute users without storing persistent cookies on their device, ensuring compliance with privacy regulations like GDPR and CCPA.
Can I use Tracked with React/Next.js?
Absolutely. We offer a first-class React SDK and dedicated Next.js components to make integration seamless.
